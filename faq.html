FAQ – Your Questions, Answered

Practical answers for modern managers. If it’s not here, ask us.

About the Toolkit

Who is the Manager Toolkit for?

New managers, team leads, and anyone who wants practical, no-BS leadership tools. If you lead people, this is for you.

Is this theory or real-world advice?

Real-world all the way. You’ll get templates, scripts, and frameworks you can use the same day.

Using the Toolkit

How do I use it day to day?

Download, print, or pull it up on your phone. It’s designed for quick grabs between meetings—clarity over complexity.

Will this work in a very “old-school” company?

Yes. People are people. The Toolkit focuses on clarity, trust, and practical scripts that work across hybrid, remote, and traditional teams.

Is it mobile-friendly?

Absolutely. Everything renders cleanly on phones so you can discreetly get what you need and handle your business.

Access & Delivery

How do I get access?

Join the early access list and you’ll be first to receive it. Early supporters may get extras and bonuses.

Will there be app-style access?

Yes. We’re building a Progressive Web App so you can install Agravic Shift on your phone for quick tips, offline access, and (optionally) daily reminders.

Value & Cost

Is it free?

Early access materials are free. The full Toolkit will be a paid resource with tiers (digital, printable, and premium add-ons like the app & assistant).

Why not just Google this stuff?

You could—but Google gives you noise. The Toolkit is curated, modern, and practical so you save time and avoid trial-and-error.

Support

What if I need more help?

We’ve got you. You’ll be able to tap the Agravic Assistant (our AI advisor) for on-demand support, plus email us anytime at support@agravicshift.com.

Is this HR or legal advice?

No. It’s practical guidance for managers. For policy-specific or legal issues, check with your HR/Legal team.
